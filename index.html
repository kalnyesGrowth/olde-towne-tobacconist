Our Story

Tobacco Is Our Passion

Olde Towne Tobacconist is a proper tobacconist in the heart of historic downtown Fredericksburg. We carry an excellent selection of premium cigars, pipes, pipe tobacco, and a wide assortment of smoking accessories and unique gifts.

Our staff are cigar smokers. We can help you find the smoke you're describing, and we make great suggestions too. Whether you're a seasoned aficionado or picking up your first stick, you'll feel welcome here.

Stop by and have a look around, enjoy a smoke in our courtyard or lounge, and walk the streets of this historic Civil War city while enjoying your favorite smoke.

Questions

Frequently Asked

What brands of cigars do you carry?

We carry over 30 premium brands including Arturo Fuente, Padron, Drew Estate, La Flor Dominicana, Rocky Patel, My Father, Oliva, Ashton, CAO, E.P. Carrillo, Perdomo, Camacho, Kristoff, La Gloria Cubana, Room 101, AVO, La Aurora, and many more. Our stock changes regularly with new releases and special orders.

Do you have a place to sit and smoke?

Yes. We offer two indoor smoking lounges with comfortable seating and a wide-screen TV, plus the biggest outdoor courtyard in downtown Fredericksburg. Everyone is welcome to enjoy a smoke with us.

Can you help me choose a cigar if I'm new?

Absolutely. Our staff are cigar smokers ourselves. We love to help customers find the right smoke, whether you're a seasoned aficionado or just getting started. Tell us what flavors you enjoy and we'll guide you to a great first cigar.

Do you carry pipe tobacco?

Yes, we carry pipe tobacco from Lane, Peter Stokkebye, and other quality producers. We also have a selection of pipes and pipe accessories. If you're looking for something specific, call ahead and we'll check availability.

Can you special order cigars?

If we don't have what you're looking for in stock, ask us about special orders. We're happy to try to get specific cigars or brands for our customers.

Do you host events?

We regularly host Bourbon and Cigars nights, cigar launches, blender events, movie nights, and BBQs in our courtyard. Check our events page or follow us on social media for the latest schedule.

Where are you located?

We're at 719-A Caroline Street in historic downtown Fredericksburg, VA, right across the street from the Visitor's Center. You can't miss us. Free street parking and nearby garages are available.

What are your hours?

Monday through Wednesday 10 AM to 8 PM, Thursday through Saturday we're open until 10 PM (Friday and Saturday we open at 9 AM), and Sunday 10:30 AM to 6 PM.
